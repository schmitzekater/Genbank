This layout has been tested successfully on a PC running Mozilla 1.7.5, Netscape 7.1 (yes even NN), FireFox 1.0.3, Opera 8, Internet Explorer IE5.01, IE5.5 and IE6. It resizes down to almost nothing and adds scroll bars when necessary, the vertical scroll bar even stays on screen.

Any feedback on Mac browser compatibility would be nice.

Latest news ~ Works on Mac, Safari (1.3) and FireFox (1.0.3) - Thanks Todd.

The fixed left navigation will get lost under the footer with very small screen sizes and would probably look better in the top header or as part of the body scrolling area.
